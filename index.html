About Me

Hello! I am a senior undergradate student majoring in Data Science at Fudan Univeristy, China. I also write blog posts about how machine learning practitioners can use techniques that are of interest in the mathematical optimization research community. From talking to people who use machine learning in industry, I’ve found that there’s a gap between what gets used in industry and what gets studied in academia, and I hope to help bring the two closer together.

Research Interests

In general, I study nonconvex optimization techniques for machine learning. My work recently has been on Matrix Completion and Robust PCA, with applications in data analysis for power systems. Previously, I have done research on nonconvex optimization techniques in Bioinformatics, which is something I hope to do more of in the future
